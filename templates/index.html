Tips for best results

  • Use sharp, well-lit photos (avoid blur or heavy shadows).
  • Include a single leaf in frame against a plain background.
  • Center the diseased region if visible.
  • Capture both sides of the leaf if possible.
  • Avoid including other plants or objects in the image.

How it works

Our deep learning model analyzes leaf images to detect common plant diseases with high accuracy. The system provides diagnosis and suggested remedies to help maintain plant health.
